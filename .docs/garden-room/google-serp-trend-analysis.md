## 2. Key Ranking Trends Identified

### 2.1 Google Strongly Favours Irish-Domiciled, Industry-Specific Domains

- **10 out of 11** results are `.ie` domains — Google shows extreme local preference for this query.
- The sole `.com` (gardenroomdublin.com) is an **exact-match domain (EMD)** containing "garden room" + "Dublin", which historically carries strong weight for local intent queries.
- **No international brands** rank. No UK-based companies, no Amazon/marketplace listings. This is a purely local service SERP.

**Implication for Modular House**: The `modularhouse.ie` or similar `.ie` domain is critical. Ensure the primary garden room landing page lives on the `.ie` domain.

---

### 2.2 Page Title Patterns — Location + Product + Differentiator

Google rewards titles that combine:

| Element | Example | Frequency |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| **Primary keyword** ("Garden Room/Rooms") | All 11 results | 11/11 |
| **Geographic qualifier** ("Ireland" or "Dublin") | "Garden Rooms Ireland", "Dublin" | 9/11 |
| **Differentiator / USP** | "Ireland's Original", "Best", "Insulated", "Home Office", "Eco" | 8/11 |
| **Brand name** | "MCD", "Shomera", "NUA" | 7/11 |

**Winning formula**: `[Primary Keyword] [Location] | [USP/Differentiator] – [Brand]`
- Example: _"Garden Rooms Dublin | Steel Frame & Insulated – Modular House"_

### 2.4 Content Structure Patterns on Top-Ranking Pages

Analysing the actual page content reveals a consistent structure Google rewards:

#### Tier 1 — Above the Fold (Must-Have)
1. **Hero section** with strong H1 containing "Garden Room(s)" + location
2. **Immediate social proof** (years in business, number of builds, star ratings)
3. **Primary CTA** (quote request, phone number, or WhatsApp)

#### Tier 2 — Product Showcase
4. **Product range/catalogue** with clear size options, images, and pricing (where applicable)
5. **Named product lines** or categorisation system (e.g., Classic vs Contemporary, Gold vs Silver, Cedar vs Charred Larch)
6. **Multiple use cases** explicitly listed (home office, gym, yoga studio, bar, playroom, music room)

#### Tier 3 — Trust & Authority
7. **Customer testimonials/reviews** with names and star ratings (Google Reviews or Trustpilot widgets)
8. **Project gallery** with completed installation photos
9. **"Why choose us"** section with bullet-pointed differentiators
10. **Process/steps** section (typically 3-step: enquire → design → build)

#### Tier 4 — Supporting SEO Content
11. **FAQ section** addressing planning permission, base requirements, timeline, pricing
12. **Technical specifications** (insulation values, U-values, materials, dimensions)
13. **Long-form descriptive copy** about what makes a great garden room
14. **Contact/enquiry form** with location details

## 3. Competitive Landscape Segmentation

The top 11 results can be grouped into three distinct business models:

### Segment A: Premium Bespoke Builders (Positions 2, 3, 7, 8, 11)
- **gardenrooms.ie, shomera.ie, gardenroomdublin.com, roomsoutside.ie, nuagardenrooms.ie**
- Offer fully custom, turnkey garden rooms
- Higher price points (€20K–€50K+)
- Reliant on consultations and site surveys
- Heavy on brand storytelling, testimonials, and design credentials
- **Content-rich** — long-form copy, use-case descriptions, "why choose us", process explanations

### Segment B: Manufacturer / Shed-to-Garden-Room (Positions 1, 6)
- **mcdgardensheds.ie, shanettesheds.ie**
- Origin in garden sheds/garages, expanded into insulated garden rooms
- Steel or timber frame construction
- Mid-range pricing
- Strong on **product specifications** (insulation, U-values, materials)
- Multiple product tiers (Gold, Silver, Bronze range names)

### Segment C: E-Commerce / Product Catalogue (Positions 4, 5, 10)
- **loghouse.ie, summerhouse24.ie, shedfactoryireland.ie**
- Large product catalogues (50–150+ SKUs)
- Full e-commerce with "Add to basket"
- Lower price points (€5K–€30K)
- Often flat-pack / DIY / kit-based
- Less emphasis on bespoke design, more on product volume and filtering

## 4. What Google is Ultimately Rewarding

Based on the analysis, Google ranks "garden room" in Dublin according to these prioritised signals:

### Tier 1 (Essential)
1. **Local relevance** — .ie domain, Dublin/Ireland in content, Google Business Profile
2. **Topical authority** — "garden room(s)" in URL, H1, title, throughout content
3. **Domain authority** — established brands, review signals, backlink profiles

### Tier 2 (Strong Differentiators)
4. **Comprehensive content** — addressing the full user journey (research → compare → enquire)
5. **Trust signals** — testimonials, review widgets, years in business, number of builds
6. **Use-case diversity** — addressing multiple intents (office, gym, entertainment, etc.)

### Tier 3 (Emerging / Tactical)
7. **Product structured data** — largely missing across competitors; first-mover advantage available
8. **Pricing signals** — transparent pricing appears correlated with ranking for e-commerce models
9. **Visual content** — gallery images, project photos, video (rare in competitor set)

## 7. Summary: What Top-Ranking Garden Room Sites Have in Common

1. **Strong `.ie` local domains** with garden room keywords in URL paths
2. **Title tags combining** primary keyword + Ireland/Dublin + brand/USP
3. **Hero sections** with immediate social proof and clear CTA
4. **Product catalogues** with sizes, images, and often pricing
5. **Multiple use-case coverage** (office, gym, studio, bar, playroom, etc.)
6. **Customer testimonials** prominently displayed with ratings
7. **FAQ sections** addressing planning permission, pricing, and process
8. **Technical specification content** about insulation, materials, and guarantees
9. **Physical showroom presence** in Dublin mentioned prominently
10. **Long-form authority content** demonstrating E-E-A-T

**The single biggest gap across all 11 competitors**: None utilise structured data (schema markup) to its full potential. Implementing `Product`, `FAQPage`, `LocalBusiness`, `AggregateRating`, and `BreadcrumbList` schema gives Modular House a technical SEO advantage that none of the current top 11 possess.
