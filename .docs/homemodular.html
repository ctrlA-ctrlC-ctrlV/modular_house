Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take from order to installation?

Timelines depend on your site and the model, but installation is designed to be fast. In many cases, delivery, groundwork, and utilities can be completed in around two weeks.

 

What is included in the turnkey price?

Your unit comes fully fitted with a kitchen and bathroom, with appliances included, plus delivery and on site installation.

Do you handle groundworks and service connections?

Yes. We manage site preparation and coordinate connections so you are not organising multiple contractors.

Can I change the layout, windows, or doors?

Yes. Layout flexibility is part of the offer. Options depend on the model and site requirements.

Do I need planning permission?

Planning varies by location, intended use, and how the unit is placed. We will guide you through the typical considerations and what your local authority may ask for.

What do I need on site before delivery?

Access for delivery, a prepared base, and a plan for services. We will confirm what is needed during the site review.

Are the units suitable for the Irish climate?

Yes. The build focuses on strong insulation and energy performance for year round comfort.

What happens after handover?

You get aftercare support from the same team, so any questions or minor adjustments can be handled quickly.
