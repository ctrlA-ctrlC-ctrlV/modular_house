# Garden Room Page - Competitor Analysis

## Competitors Analyzed (Top Google Results: "garden room" in Dublin, Ireland)

| Site | Positioning | Key Strength |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| gardenrooms.ie | "Ireland's Original" (est. 2003) | Brand authority, Google Reviews badge, named product ranges |
| mygardenroom.ie | "Best Value" | Polished Squarespace design, material differentiation (Duraboard, QuickCast) |
| shanettesheds.ie | Blog/content-led | HubSpot CMS, content marketing strategy |
| shomera.ie | Design-focused premium | Most comprehensive schema markup, video content, detailed explanatory copy |

---

## Common Page Structure (All 4 Competitors)

1. **Hero** - Full-width image/slideshow + headline + CTA
2. **Product Ranges** - Named product lines (Cube, Ultimate, Classic) with "View Range" CTAs
3. **Gallery/Portfolio** - Carousel or grid of completed projects
4. **Trust Signals** - Testimonials, review badges, years in business
5. **Explanatory Content** - "What Makes a Great Garden Room?" long-form copy
6. **Video** - Short-form (15-49s) embedded Vimeo/YouTube
7. **FAQ Section** - Common questions (planning permission, timeline, pricing)
8. **Contact/CTA** - Lead capture form with use-case dropdown

---

## SEO Patterns Observed

### Meta Tags
- Title tags: 50-90 chars, benefit-oriented ("Ireland's Original Garden Rooms")
- Descriptions: under 160 chars, mention product types + brand positioning
- All implement OG + Twitter cards fully

### Structured Data
- Organization/LocalBusiness schema (most sites)
- WebSite + WebPage schema (gardenrooms.ie, shomera.ie)
- VideoObject schema (shomera.ie only)
- **Missing across ALL**: FAQPage, Product, Review/AggregateRating, BreadcrumbList

### Content Strategy
- Multiple H2s targeting long-tail keywords
- Product range names as anchor points
- "Why choose us" / "What makes us different" sections
- Location-specific mentions (Dublin, Ireland)

---

## Key Competitive Gaps We Can Exploit

1. **No competitor uses Product schema** - We can be first with structured pricing/size data
2. **No competitor uses FAQPage schema** - Despite all having FAQ content
3. **No competitor segments by size** - All use branded names (Cube, Classic) rather than m² sizing
4. **No competitor addresses the 45m² legislation** - Fresh content opportunity
5. **Limited video content** - Only shomera.ie uses video
6. **No interactive product comparison** - All use static content only

---

## Takeaways for Modular House

- **Product differentiation**: Size-based categorization (15/25/35/45m²) is unique in market
- **SEO opportunity**: FAQPage + Product schema = potential rich snippets ahead of competitors
- **Content gap**: Planning permission legislation content is unaddressed by competitors
- **Trust building**: Competitors rely heavily on testimonials and review badges
- **CTA placement**: Multiple CTAs throughout page (not just hero and footer)
